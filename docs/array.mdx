## TC of insert in vectors
Now you know what a vector is, but do you know about the time complexity of insert operation in a vector. For arrays it is but simple O(1), is it the same case with vectors, what do you think? Well here is a spoiler, the TC is not exactly O(1) but the amortised TC is O(1). So what does it mean and what is the significance, read on and you will find out. First there is introduction of Amortised TC followed by TC of insert in vectors.
